Our Story

Who We Are

Premier Herbal Inc. has been supplying premium herbs, spices, and botanical ingredients to Canadian businesses since 1999. Based in Toronto, we work with manufacturers, retailers, cosmetic brands, tea blenders, and artisan producers who demand consistent quality and reliable supply.

Our herbs and spices are not genetically engineered and not irradiated. We maintain rigorous quality standards to ensure every product meets the exacting expectations of our clients — from small artisan producers to industry leaders.

Proudly supplying Naturopaths, Distilleries, Cosmetics companies, Tea companies, and Pet food companies across Canada.

Founder & Owner: Kay Vohra, Ph.D.
